About Us

DnA is a collaboration between Dana Amundsen and Alex O’Keefe, midwestern zinsters, illustrators, and comic artists. Dana draws girls with swords (and cats), while Alex draws nature goth illustrations (and bunnies). They’ve been drawing together since 2010 no matter how far apart they find themselves.
